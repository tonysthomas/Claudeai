Strategic brand & growth plan

It's AI Education — Strategy

Close the gap between AI adoption and AI understanding. 75% of knowledge workers use AI at work; only 39% have had any training. Governance is the wedge — the weakest pillar across every competitor, with a regulatory deadline behind it.

Purpose

Close the gap between AI adoption and AI understanding. Teach the people most exposed — managers, HR, teachers, retail staff, SME owners — properly, in plain English, one small lesson at a time.

Vision

Become the trusted plain-English authority on AI fluency & governance for non-technical professionals and SMEs across the GCC and beyond — from a faceless Instagram page into a face-led education platform delivering ebooks, consulting and courses from Dubai.

Mission

Make non-technical people confident and safe with AI. Every post solves exactly one small problem. Explain clearly what everyone else overcomplicates. Never hype, never fear, never jargon.

Strategy

Masses-first for reach, SME-adjacent for revenue. Instagram stays faceless and builds reach; LinkedIn carries the face and closes the money; the email list is the asset that outlives both.
